Do you like circus?

We are happy to announce you the opening of Wildcircus in Orleans !
What is the wildcircus? The wildcircus is a mixed young troupe of artist wich proposes interactive shows with the public.
You will be invited to participate during the show!
Whether with clowns, animals or magicians when you enter the WildCircus you are guaranteed to have fun!

Who are we?

In reality we are not confirmed artists ... Our troupe is composed of student of circus schools who come to train by realizing show. But don't worry they are not amateurs !! You will not see the difference with confirms artist. You will meet ten clowns who will do everything to be serious, magicians who will amaze you with their magic tricks , even if sometimes one asks if a clown has not hidden among them ...
And of course the majestic animals of the savannah will make you travel!
